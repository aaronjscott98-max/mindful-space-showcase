This project originates from my honours work, which was officially marked and graded back in 2023. To be completely clear, this revamped version is a personal initiative and is not intended to alter or replace that original academic grading in any way.

Instead, I decided to return to the project three years after graduating to breathe some new life into it with fresh design changes and a functional website build. My goal is to give the concept an upgraded feel and to see just how far I have come as a designer and developer since finishing the original project.

Now officially titled Mindful Space a name that feels appropriate for this type of serious subject matter the original work focused on spreading awareness of anxiety and depression among students during COVID-19, alongside the impact of the cost of living crisis on those balancing work and coursework. This evolved project will build on that foundation with additional research to explore what has changed and if anything has changed since then.
